About Us

Gamexia is an after-school activity program designed to make learning more fun and engaging, improving retention and understanding.

Dyslexia is a neurobiological condition that affects 1 in 5 children worldwide. It causes difficulties with accurate and fluent word recognition, spelling, and decoding. Traditional learning environments often fail to meet their needs.

For most literate individuals, reading happens automatically. A popular internet message illustrates how easily people read even when letters are scrambled:
